Metallica (a.k.a The Black Album)

Metallica

August 12, 1991

Metallica (commonly know as The Black Album) is the self-titled fifth studio album by American heavy metal band Metallica. The album received widespread critical acclaim and became the band's best-selling album. The album is ranked 255 on Rolling Stone's 500 greatest albums of all time.

Band Members and Producers

James Hetfield - Lead vocals, rhythm guitar, production

Kirk Hammett - Lead Guitar

Jason Newsted - Bass

Lars Ulrich - Drums, production

Michael Kamen - Orchestral arrangement on Nothing Else Matters
